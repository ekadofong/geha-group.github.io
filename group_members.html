Dr. Marla Geha

Principal Investigator

Marla Geha is a full professor at Yale University. She obtained a B.S. in Applied and Engineering Physics from Cornell University and a PhD in Astrophysics from University of California Santa Cruz. She has received honors including an Alfred P. Sloan Research Fellowship, a John S. Guggenheim Fellowship and the Dylan Hixon '88 Prize for teaching excellence in the natural sciences at Yale. She is in the board of the Warrior Scholar Project and runs the REVU summer research program.
